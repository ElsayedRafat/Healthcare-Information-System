WHO ARE WE

Benha Medical Center

Benha Medical Center Group has expanded throughout the Kingdom of Saudi Arabia, after proving its excellence in terms of quality and scientific management. With many patients traveling to Germany in search of the highest standards of medical care, these patients and their families faced many challenges to locate centers that suited their health conditions, as well as cultural and logistical obstacles. Therefore, the Batterjee family decided to establish its first hospital in Jeddah, through its strategic relations with the most famous German university in the field of healthcare.
